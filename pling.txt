Fork of Bibata cursors using the Catppuccin color theme.

Check [url=https://github.com/Treecase/bibata-cursor-catppuccin]README.md[/url] for installation.

[b]Bibata Styles:[/b]
- Bibata Modern: Round Edges Bibata Cursors
- Bibata Original: Sharp Edges Bibata Cursors

[b]XCursor Sizes:[/b]
16x16, 20x20, 22x22, 24x24, 28x28, 32x32, 40x40, 48x48, 56x56, 64x64, 72x72, 80x80, 88x88, 96x96

[b]Windows Cursor Size:[/b]
- 16x16 - Small
- 24x24 - Regular
- 32x32 - Large
- 48x48 - Extra Large
